ABOUT OUR RADIO STATION


Our Vision

Broadcasting services with commitment to programmes of local relevance, talent and events coverage based on a pluralistic approach enabling broad programming coverage.

Our Mission

Gtfm 104.8 strives to facilitate development through a community radio station that will disseminate information and educate the communities of the area on issues that will enhance their standard of living and improve their quality of life.
